RythuLedger is a separate app from MandiLedger, built for **farmers and managing partners** who run their own plots. Field expenses, labour, harvest, and partner accounts, not mandi trip/settlement workflows.

The product direction came from real spreadsheets: multi-plot expense ledgers with partner splits, crop yield records, cutting/harvest sales, and balance sheets. The goal is to digitize that mental model in a **neat, Telugu-friendly, offline-first** mobile app.

## What it does today

- **Labour books**: laborers, daily work logs, advances and deductions, wage settlements
- **Farm books**: plots, seasons, field expenses with owner/joint/partner splits, partner balances, harvest cuttings, cross-crop transfers
- **Plot map**: boundaries, plant grids, irrigation assets (valve, motor, drip), health filters
- **Reports**: filtered SQL exports for labour and farm data to CSV and XLSX
- **CSV import**: Pulivendula-style field expense and cutting spreadsheets
- **i18n**: English, Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Marathi, Odia, Malayalam, Kannada

## Architecture

```text
Expo app (expo-router + SQLite)
        ↓ local queue
Cloud sync API (planned: /rythu/farm/*, /rythu/labor/*)
```

Ported UI patterns and shared types from MandiLedger, but a **separate app** with its own package id (`com.rythuledger.app`) and product scope. MandiLedger handles mandi/distributor workflows; RythuLedger handles on-farm accounting.

## Roadmap

Still early. The labour module works offline; farm books are actively expanding. Next priorities: plots and seasons CRUD, field expense entry, harvest/cutting P&L, partner ledger settlement math, Firebase auth, and cloud backup.

## Why I built it

My uncle runs decades of farm accounting in Excel. RythuLedger is the path from those sheets to something he can use in the field on spotty connectivity: one thumb, under a minute to log today's tractor hire.
