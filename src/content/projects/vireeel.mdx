Vireeel generates user-generated-content style advertisements using multimodal AI — scripts, visuals, and assembly pipelines for short-form platforms.

## Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js for the creator dashboard
- **Backend**: FastAPI for orchestration and asset pipelines
- **Models**: Gemini for script and creative generation

## What I learned

Building a product around generative AI means designing for failure modes: hallucinated claims, inconsistent brand voice, and latency spikes during peak generation.
