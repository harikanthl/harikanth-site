Patrika is a SwiftUI, SwiftData-based life workspace — not just a journal, but a calm daily operating system for reflection, habits, health, and focus.

## Journal

- Rich content entries with inline text, photos, GIFs, Lottie animations, and stickers
- Text styling — bold, italic, headings, quotes, bullet and numbered lists
- Voice journaling with multilingual speech transcription and on-device recognition where available
- Sentiment analysis, keyword extraction, and semantic search over entries
- A Lottie-based companion that reflects your emotional state

## Day workspace

Beyond journaling, Patrika bundles adjacent tools into a single day view:

- **Habits** — daily tracking with heat maps and pattern insights
- **Todos** — lightweight task management tied to the calendar
- **Expenses** — budget tracking with monthly rollups
- **Focus** — timer sessions for deep work
- **Health** — medication logs, diet entries, water tracking, exercise plans
- **Reset zone** — meditation, walks, reflection, and talk-it-out interventions
- **Trips** — itinerary planning with AI-assisted suggestions
- **Brain rot** — Screen Time integration showing app usage breakdowns

## On-device intelligence

Most analysis runs locally — emotion detection, semantic search, weekly insights, habit patterns, expense insights, handwriting recognition from canvas drawings, and foundation-model reflection.

## Why I built it

I wanted one app that holds the messy parts of a day — what I wrote, what I ate, whether I moved, how much time I spent on my phone — without sending all of it to a cloud service.
